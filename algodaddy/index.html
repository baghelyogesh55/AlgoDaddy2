Important tips

Try to focus on these three things when you do something new.

What do you want to do?

Be clear with what you want to do and why do you want to do. Have a strong motivation to complete it to your fullest and whenever you feel dull, take rest but don't quit.

How do you want to do?

Make a road map of how you are going to accomplish your task. Mark all the possible circumstances of which phases you will be going through. And decide what things to do first. Set your priorities.

When are you finishing it?

Keep a dealine to finish your work or else you would find difficulty in finishing the work you started and you will feel dull. Keep yourself motivating and confident to complete it before your decided deadline.
